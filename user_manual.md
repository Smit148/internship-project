# 📚 Nexo E-commerce: End User Manual

Welcome to the Nexo E-commerce platform documentation. This guide is designed to help all users—from everyday customers to internal staff and HR administrators—navigate and utilize the system effectively.

---

## 🛍️ 1. Customer Guide

This section is for standard users who want to browse, shop, and manage their orders on the platform.

### Account Management
*   **Registration**: Click **Register** in the top navigation bar. Enter your name, email, and a secure password.
*   **Login**: Click **Login** and enter your credentials. You can also use the **Demo User** button for testing.
*   **Logout**: When logged in, click the **Logout** button under your profile name in the top right.

### Shopping Experience
*   **Browsing Products**: Click **Products** in the navigation bar to see the catalog. You can view prices, stock levels, and category badges.
*   **Wishlist**: See an item you like but want to buy later? Click the ❤️ icon on a product card to save it. Access your saved items anytime via the **Wishlist** tab. *Note: Wishlist items are securely tied to your specific account.*
*   **Cart Management**: Click **Add to Cart** on any product. Access your cart via the 🛒 icon in the top right. You can adjust quantities or remove items before proceeding to checkout.

### Checkout & Orders
*   **Placing an Order**: From the Cart, click **Checkout**. You will go through three steps:
    1.  **Delivery Address**: Enter your shipping details.
    2.  **Payment Method**: Select from Cash on Delivery (COD), UPI, Card, or Wallet.
    3.  **Confirmation**: Review your final invoice and confirm.
*   **Order History**: Click **Orders** in the navigation bar to track the status of your past purchases.

## 🏬 2. Staff Guide (Store Operations)

This section is for **Staff** and **Employees** responsible for managing the store's inventory, product categories, and incoming customer orders.

### Accessing the Staff Portal
1. Log in using a Staff account (or use the **Employee Demo** login button).
2. Click the **Staff Portal** button that appears in the top navigation bar.

### Managing Categories
Categories organize the store's products (e.g., *Electronics, Footwear*).
*   **Create**: Click **+ Add Category**. Enter the name and description.
*   **Edit**: Click **Edit** next to an existing category to update its details.
*   **Deactivate (Soft Delete)**: You can mark a category as inactive. 
    > [!WARNING]
    > If you attempt to deactivate a category that still has products assigned to it, the system will issue a warning. You must reassign those products to prevent storefront display errors.

### Managing Products
*   **Inventory Control**: Navigate to the **Products** tab. Here you can view current stock levels. Items with 0 stock are highlighted in red.
*   **Add/Edit**: You can create new products, set their pricing, assign them to categories, and toggle their active status.

### Managing Orders
*   **Order Fulfillment**: Navigate to the **Orders** tab to view all customer purchases. You can update the status of an order from *Pending* to *Processing*, *Shipped*, or *Delivered*.

## 👥 3. HR Guide (Employee Management)

This section is for **HR Administrators** responsible for onboarding and managing internal platform staff.

### Accessing the HR Panel
1. Log in using an Admin or HR account (or use the **Admin Demo** login button).
2. Click the **HR Portal** button in the top navigation bar.

### Employee Management
*   **View Directory**: The dashboard provides a comprehensive table of all registered employees, displaying their roles and contact information.
*   **Onboarding**: Click **+ Register Employee** to add new staff members to the system, granting them access to the Staff Portal.

---

> [!TIP]
> **Need Help?** 
> If you experience any `404 Not Found` errors when navigating, please ensure you are logged into an account with the correct permissions (e.g., attempting to access the HR Portal as a standard customer will not work).

## 💻 4. Core Management Modules

The platform includes several core management modules accessible to administrators for seamless operations.

### Customer Management Module
- **Dashboard**: View all registered customers, their contact details, and order counts.
- **Add/Edit**: Manually add or edit customer details.
- **Soft Delete**: Deactivate customer accounts without permanently deleting their data, maintaining order history integrity.

### Payment Management Module
- **Dashboard**: View all transaction history, including payment IDs, order IDs, methods (Card, PayPal, etc.), and status.
- **Refunds**: Process refunds for cancelled orders directly from the dashboard.
- **Analytics**: Track total revenue, refunded amounts, and failed transactions.

### Cart Management Module
- **Dashboard**: Monitor active, abandoned, and converted carts.
- **Abandonment Analysis**: Track cart abandonment rates to improve checkout conversion.
- **Management**: Admins can view contents of customer carts and remove items if necessary.

### Wishlist Management Module
- **Dashboard**: Track popular products that users are saving for later.
- **Analytics**: View the most wishlisted products and the number of unique customers using the feature.
- **Management**: Monitor wishlist entries and remove invalid records.

### Shipping Management Module
- **Dashboard**: Track all shipments across the platform.
- **Cost Calculation**: View shipping costs applied to orders.
- **Tracking Updates**: Admins can update courier services, tracking numbers, and delivery statuses (Pending, Shipped, In Transit, Delivered) for any order.
